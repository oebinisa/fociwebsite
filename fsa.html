Mission

To provide unique hand-on training on construction skills through Dual Technical and Vocational training to promote social equity, youth empowerment and sustainable development.







FOCI Skills Academy, FSA is a national provider of vocational training for the construction industry under the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) developed by the National Board for Technical Education, NBTE. FSA is positioned to be the most innovative provider of construction skills in Nigeria.

The academy was registered in 2019 by the Federation of Construction Industry, FOCI to address the following challenges:

  • High rate of employment among the youth
  • Shortage of skilled artisans for construction activities
  • Limited access to skills development opportunities especially for women
  • Large number of unskilled construction graduates
  • The problem of skill-mismatch because of obsolete curriculum

Objectives

The objectives of FSA include but not limited to the followings:

  • Training and certification of apprentices
  • Upskilling of artisans and craftsmen
  • Short term training of staff of construction companies
  • Re-skilling of interested individuals.
  • Training the Trainer
  • Holiday exposures and practical exposures for secondary school students and undergraduates/graduates of tertiary institutions
  • Specialized training and consultancy services

Training Partners

  • GIZ/SEQUA:
    • FOCI signed a collaborative agreement with the German Agency for International Cooperation, GIZ. Through the Agreement GIZ provides support for training of trainers and curriculum development and provision of resident integrated skills expert.

  • Member Companies:
    • FOCI have over 80 active construction companies whose site are available for in-company training, The trainees would be mentored by skilled foremen and supervisors.

  • One year Competency Based Dual Vocational training in Carpentry & joinery.
  • One year Competency Based Dual Vocational training in Plumbing& fittings.

  • Six (6) months Competency Based Dual Vocational training in Carpentry & joinery works

  • Three (3) months Basic Masonry works (Concreting and stone works)

  • Three (3) months Basic Electrical works

International Projects

  • UNESCO-BILT:
    • FSA was selected by UNESCO as one of the eight (8) institutions from Africa, Asia, and Europe to participate on the Bridging innovation and learning in TVET (BILT Project) to co-develop and pilot the New Qualification and Competencies Self Reflection Tool. Our Innovation and Learning Practice (ILP) was published on UNESCO Website foci_Nigeria (unesco.org)

  • GIZ/SEQUA:
    • FSA with the support of GIZ/Sequa has successfully developed Competency-Based curricula for construction trades. These curricula have been tested and validated by NBTE and are now available for the use of the public.
