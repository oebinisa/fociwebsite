FOCI Scholarship



About the Scheme

FOCI also allow two special categories of membership:

FOCI instituted a Scholarship Award in memory of the Late General Murtala Mohammed in 1976. It is a scheme targeted at students in Building Technology and civil Engineering degree programmes in tertiary institutions in Nigeria.

The Murtala Ramat Muhammed Scholarship Fund is a major component of FOCI's Corporate Social Responsibilities in the strategic development of Building and Civil Engineering in Nigeria.

We are the reference point for sustained excellence in the Nigerian Construction Industry. FOCI's interaction with Member companies has resulted in the maintenance of sound practices, respect for ethics and stability.
